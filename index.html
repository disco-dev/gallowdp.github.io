The Elegant Plums

The Elegant Plums are a four-piece band out of Denver, Colorado. Members of the band have influence from all realms of the music world and have brought those sounds together to create a new blend of many genres, from blues to funk, with each song based around improvisational jams. To know The Elegant Plums one must experience their fun blend of creative covers and diverse originals that can turn any normal evening into an elegant one.
